Maniesh Kumar Choudhary

Managing Partner

Maniesh Kumar Choudhary completed LL.B. in the year 1994 from Delhi University and has been actively practicing since last 24 years at the Supreme Court. In the course of overall period and practice, he had the occasion to interact and deal with various leading legal luminaries in the field of law such as Dr. L.M. Singhvi, Dr. A.K. Sen, Sh. Ashok Desai, Sh. Soli Sorabjee, Sh. U.R. Lalit, Sh. Dushyant Dave, Sh. Arun Jaitley, Sh. A.M. Singhvi, Sh. R.F. Nariman, Sh. V.A. Mohta, Sh. Mukul Rohatgi, Sh. Nagendra Rai, Sh. Ranjeet Kumar, Sh. Kapil Sibbal, Sh. P. Chidambram, etc. and assisted them in various cases. He is empanelled with various private concerns as well as to different Govt. bodies.

He has experience of handling various nature of cases, i.e. Civil, Criminal, Consumer, Arbitration, Company, Corporate, Service, Trademark and Patent etc. at various level starting from the lower Courts up to the Hon’ble Supreme Court and has also the added experience of assisting the various leading serious advocates right from lower courts to Hon’ble Supreme Court.
